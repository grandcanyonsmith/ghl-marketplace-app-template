‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the app in my GHL account?
Visit the GoHighLevel Marketplace, search for our app, and click "Install". You'll be guided through a simple setup process that takes less than 5 minutes.
What permissions does the app need?
We only request the minimum permissions needed: read/write access to contacts and leads, automation workflows, and dashboard analytics. We never access sensitive data like billing information.
Can I customize the dashboard widgets?
Yes! You can add, remove, and rearrange dashboard widgets to match your workflow. Go to Settings ‚Üí Dashboard to customize your view.
How do I create my first automation?
Navigate to the Automation section, click "Create New Workflow", choose a trigger (like form submission), add actions (send email, SMS, etc.), and activate. We also provide templates for common scenarios.
Is the app mobile-friendly?
Absolutely! Our app is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. You can access all features, view analytics, and manage automations from any device.
What happens to my data if I uninstall the app?
Your GHL data remains untouched. We only store app-specific settings and preferences, which are permanently deleted within 30 days of uninstallation per our privacy policy.
Do you offer API access for custom integrations?
Yes! We provide a comprehensive REST API for custom integrations. Check our API documentation for endpoints and authentication details.
Can I export my automation data and analytics?
Yes, you can export data in CSV, JSON, or PDF formats. Go to Settings ‚Üí Export Data to download your automation performance metrics and analytics reports.
